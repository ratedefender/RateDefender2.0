What is Purchasing Power Parity (PPP)?

Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) is an economic theory suggesting that exchange rates between currencies should equalize the purchasing power of different currencies. In simpler terms: $100 in Mumbai buys significantly more goods and services than $100 in New York. The PPP Rate Adjuster tool helps you calculate a fair rate when working with clients in high-cost-of-living countries while you operate from lower-cost regions.
